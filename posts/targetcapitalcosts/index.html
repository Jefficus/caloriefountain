    Target "Capital Costs"

    Jefferson Smith

    2023-03-26 14:19

    Comments

    GOAL: Create an estimate of all capital expenses needed to build a calorie fountain

    Explanation

    Startup cost is going to be a significant barrier to adoption, but it's hard to estimate just how big a hurdle this will be until we have some realistic numbers.

    Deliverables

    • a realistic estimate of all capital equipment costs required to start a calorie fountain, but excluding ongoing operating costs.

    Success conditions

    • consider this a success if it can be built for less than the cost of other major household appliances, such as furnace or central AC.

    Progress

    • [ ] estimate current market value of furnace and AC for 3 person home
    • [ ] estimate costs of tables, shelving, buckets, and lights

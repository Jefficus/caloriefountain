    Target "Cheaper Nutrients"

    Jefferson Smith

    2023-03-26 14:19

    Comments

    GOAL: Identify a cheaper source of hydroponic nutrient blend equivalent to the Masterblend

    Explanation

    After electric power, nutrients are the second largest expense in operating a calorie fountain, and while it's not huge, in the context of low income families, it's still significant enough to be worth trying to reduce.

    (As discussed in Targetoperatingcosts, a 12.5kg powdered fertilizer kit can supply nutrients for appx $35/m.)

    Deliverables

    • A report estimating the total monthly cost of providing nutrients by other means

    Success conditions

    • This target will be deemed a success if a practical and realistic method can be found to deliver the same nutrient profile as described in Targetoperatingcosts for $26CDN or less per month. (That's 25% cheaper.)

    Progress

    • [ ] investigate bulk purchase suppliers
    • [ ] investigate off brand and alternative suppliers
    • [ ] investigate homebrew alternatives

    Target "Kratky How"

    Jefferson Smith

    2023-03-16 10:59

    Comments

    GOAL: Determine a solid methodology for designing Kratky systems, optimizing plant densities, water volume, and nutrient formulas.

    Explanation

    We need to be able to design tank setups for the specific needs of the crop being grown.

    Deliverables

    • a method for determining optimal seedling layout
    • a method for computing initial water volume
    • a method for computing initial nutrient mix

    Success test

    • a Kratky tank designed with these rubrics produced a successful, full-term crop

    Progress

    • [x] find out how to calculate nutrient formula
    • [/] find method for estimating water needs
    • [/] write or acquire code/tool for calculating mixtures ✔
